About Me

My name is Julissa Zavala and I am a product designer and mixed media artist living in New York City.

Taking complicated problems and coming up with a solution that's not just easier to understand but also more enjoyable is one my favorite things to do.

Some of my other favorite things:

  • Hand building ceramic sculptures
  • Making everyday life more fun by listening to My Analog Journal
  • Going on long walks with my dog Penny
  • Watching reality TV but telling people it's through an 'anthropologic lens'
